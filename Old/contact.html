Are you interested on having your leaflet reviewed?

If you are developing a Patient Information Leaflet and would like to have a some feedback on it do not hestiate to contatct us. Our current beta version can help you by:

  • Exploring each sentence in your document to determine its readability with quantitative (objective) metrics
