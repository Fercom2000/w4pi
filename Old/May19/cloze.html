What is this about?

We need to assess that the document is using the correct words, it is a common case that doctors and researchers employ technical terms and little known words when initially develop a new document intended for the public. So, we employ the Cloze test to assess if the words they employ match with the words you expect to see. Sometimes the understandability of the document can be greatly improved just by replacing some synonyms.
