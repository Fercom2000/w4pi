Instructions: Please complete the following multiple choice questionnaire.

Why is this necessary?

The extended EQIP (Ensuring Quality of Information for Patients) guideline was developed to ensure that the information given to the patients is of sufficient quality to inform them. By filling this questionnaire you provide the essential information on which aspects of the documment must be improved. This is a comprehensive assessment of the information involving the employed language, topics covered, and presentation structure.
