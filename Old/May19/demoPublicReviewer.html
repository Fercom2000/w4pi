Demo: Public Reviewer Interface

This demo presents the interface employed by the public reviewers to complete 3 tasks: Assess the understandability of the text with the Cloze procedure, read and comment the leaflet's text with our W2Pi platform, and fill a questionnaire based on the EQIP guideline to assess the quality of the document.
