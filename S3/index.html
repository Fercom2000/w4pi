Welcome to the W4PI Project

This is part of a PhD project to create a Web tool to help increase the quality of medical information given to patients who are invited to join clinical trials in the UK, which has been sponsored by the University of Southampton. For this part, we want to invite you to participate by doing some simple tasks:
  1. Answer multiple choice questions
  2. Fill-in missing words to complete the sentences
  3. Help reword the sentences to make them more clear to the readers
This will help us to objectively assess how difficult to read the current information is and revise real medical documents that are being used to invite patients in joining clinical trials. By participating on this HIT you agree to let us use this information for our research. Because the collected information of this HIT will be used to revise real medical documents, submissions that are not of high quality will be rejected.
