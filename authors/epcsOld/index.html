Welcome to the Web Platform for Public Involvement

This section will present you a resume of your leaflet assessment by the public reviewers in our group.

We collected feedback from 8 public reviewers for your leaflet, of them 37.5% (3 reviewers) believe your leaflet is ready to be used, 62.5% (5 reviewers) consider minor corrections are necessary, and 0% (0 reviewers) believe you should consider a redesign.

The reviewers employed the extended EQIP guidelines to assess the information quality of your PIL. The average score given by the reviewers was 82, the Eqip recommendations are: For scores above 75% document is ready for use, above 50% can be used but you should consider doing a revision within 1-2 years, bellow 50% the PIL must be revised asap.

We also identified a some phrases which may had been to difficult to understand by your audience. We employed the Cloze procedure by omitting some of the words in the sentences, and asking the reviewers to fill in the missing words. In average only 69.6% of the assessed words in these sentences were correctly identified by the reviewers. Th Cloze procedure generates scores which are strongly correlated to the readers perception of the degree of difficulty in the narrative and the understandability of the document. It is suggested a minimum score of 57% is needed to ensure understandability.
